Flappy Bird AI: Neuroevolution

How it Works: AI & Genetic Algorithm

Genetic Algorithm: Mimics natural selection. Birds (agents) evolve over generations.

  • Selection: Best birds are chosen.
  • Crossover: Genes are mixed.
  • Mutation: Random changes introduced.

Neural Network: The brain of the bird.

  • Inputs: Bird Y, Pipe Distance, Pipe Height.
  • Hidden Layers: Process information.
  • Output: Jump or Don't Jump.
